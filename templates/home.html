Traffic lights dataset

This dataset is composed of 3000 images, 2600 of them are labeled and 400 are for testing purposes. The objective of this dataset is to identify the traffic lights in a given image. The result should be expressed giving the coordinates of the traffic light in the image, the color, and the tipe of traffic light.

This tools is constructed with the objective of visualizing more clearly what the properties of the labels mean. This is done placing the data on top of the image.

Appart from this, the filtering and sorting of the images allows a quick access to all the possible properties of the dataset.
